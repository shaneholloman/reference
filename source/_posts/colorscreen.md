## Overview {.cols-2}

### What is ColorScreen? {.col-span-2}

**[ColorScreen](https://colorscreen.co/)** is a collection of browser-based screen utilities designed for simplicity and privacy. It provides full-screen tools without ads or login requirements.

- **Utilities**: Pure color pages, monitor diagnostics, prank screens, screensavers, and timers.
- **Features**: System-level fullscreen support, local storage for settings, static web app.
- **Use Cases**: Bias lighting, display testing (dead pixels/bleed), streaming backdrops, and productivity timing.

[Visit ColorScreen.co](https://colorscreen.co/) {.link-arrow}
